About the Company

Established in 1975, Petrojet is a leading integrated EPC contractor in the Middle East and North Africa, specializing in oil, gas, petrochemicals, infrastructure, and power projects. With more than 40,000 employees and advanced in-house facilities, the company has delivered over 180 plants, 35,000 km of pipelines, 680,000 tons of storage capacity, and 300,000 tons of offshore structures. Ranked for a decade among the ENR Top 250 Global Contractors, Petrojet continues to combine technical excellence, safety, and innovation to deliver sustainable value across the region.
